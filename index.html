Pet Size Measurement Guide

Accurately measuring your pet is crucial to ensure proper clothing fit. Please follow these steps:

Measurement Steps

  1. Back Length: Length from the base of the neck (collar position) to the base of the tail
  2. Chest Girth: Circumference of the widest part behind the front legs
  3. Neck Girth: Circumference of the thickest part of the neck
  4. Weight: Accurate weight helps with size recommendations

Size Chart (Dogs)

Important Notes

Different breeds have different body proportions, so sizes may vary even with the same weight. If in doubt, refer to our interactive size assistant or contact customer service for personalized recommendations.
